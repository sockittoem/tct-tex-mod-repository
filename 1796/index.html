1796: The First of Many

by tex

1796 is considered the first mod to have both "sides" of an election complete and playable, containing both a playable Thomas Jefferson and John Adams. The mod is appropriately set in the first competitive election in American history. Although its age is apparent in many ways with repetitive questions and simplistic advisor feedback, the mod is a sure relic of an earlier, simpler time of Campaign Trail modding.
