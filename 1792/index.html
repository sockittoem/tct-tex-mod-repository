1792: The Young Republic

by tex, /u/RouteVenus and /u/Atom_H3art_Mother

Unique in having the player assume the role of a campaign manager rather than a candidate, 1792 puts the player in the role of James Madison, attempting to secure the election of George Clinton to the Vice Presidency. The mod features a large cast of widely recognised founding fathers and attempts to replicate the pre-12th Amendment system of presidential elections.
